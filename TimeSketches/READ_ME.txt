  
  These are 4 sketches for use with Arduino MKR WiFi 1010.
  Each requires you to add WiFi credentials, and your time zone

  Time_SerialMonitor uses the MKR WiFi1010 by itself.
  Time_LED uses a 4 digit 7 segment LED display.
  Time_LCD uses a 16 x 2 LCD display
  Time_Alarm uses the 4 digit 7 segment LED display with additional components to build an alarm clock
